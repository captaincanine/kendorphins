Kickstart Your Resolutions

with Kenneth Ferrer

I was done sitting on the sidelines, watching people fail at their new years resolutions

That is why I created this course. I combined my experience in business & life coaching, psychology, professional baseball, and health & fitness to create a course that will help you get clear about what you want, set a foundation for success, cultivate habits for your continuing success, and strive for more. I want to help YOU succeed in your New Years Resolution, and give you the tools to succeed in your future goals.

I love watching people get really inspired at the beginning of the new year; I find that watching someone become inspired is in itself inspirational.

But what happens when the inspiration, that is, the initial source of change-driven energy runs out? How do we become and stay motivated when we are in the midst of our goals? How do we overcome adversity when it gets in our way? What goals are realistic? I address all of these questions, and help you put your new years resolution into the context of your entire life.

In this course:

  • We will meet weekly for a 90 minute webinar which includes 60 minutes of content, and 30 minutes of Q&A. All webinar content will be available to download after class.
  • We will work through effective tools to help you set your goals, set powerful habits into place, reach your goals, and move beyond them. These tools include small homework assignments before the course starts and in between webinars.
  • We will address what gets in the way, how to deal with failure, and how to handle adversity.
  • We will stay accountable to ourselves and each other through a private, members only facebook group.
  • We will experience first-hand that when we follow through with our commitments, life opens up in a significant way.
